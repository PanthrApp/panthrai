Privacy

PantherAI collects data in order to improve our product. We aim to transparent in what data we collect and how we use it. The data we collect is constantly evolving as we improve our products. Please check back here for the latest information.

Data we collect

  • Messages: We collect messages sent to PantherAI in order to improve the services we provide. Please do not send any messages containing confidential information, as we do read through threads. We also moderate chat threads to prevent abuse.
  • Logs (eg. if an error occurs, we collect the page the error happened on.)

Data we collect but do not view

  • User account information: We will not be able to see the name or email of users of this chatbot. We store this information securely on our servers and use them only for necessary reasons such as authentication.
  • Any other data: We do not store your other data whatsoever.
